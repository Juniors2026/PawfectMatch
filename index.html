Ready to Adopt?

Fill out our adoption application form to start the process. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.

What to expect:

  • Simple online form
  • Quick response time
  • Personalized matching service

Our adoption fee ranges from $50-$200 depending on the pet, which includes spay/neuter, vaccinations, and microchipping.

Our Mission

At Pawfect Match, we believe every pet deserves a loving home and every home deserves the love of a pet. Since 2010, we've been connecting pets in need with caring families.

Our no-kill shelter provides a safe haven for animals while we work to find them permanent homes. We provide medical care, socialization, and training to prepare each animal for adoption.
